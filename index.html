Finsight Financial App

This prototype showcases a comprehensive financial app designed to help users track and manage their expenses effectively. The app, named Finsight, is crafted to provide users with insightful financial data and tools to enhance their financial health.

Key Features:

  • Expense Tracking: Users can log and categorize their expenses to monitor spending habits.
  • Budget Management: The app allows users to set budgets for different categories and track their progress.
  • Financial Insights: Provides detailed reports and visualizations to help users understand their financial status.
  • Goal Setting: Users can set financial goals and track their progress towards achieving them.
  • Notifications: Sends alerts for upcoming bills, budget limits, and financial milestones.
  • Secure Data: Ensures user data is protected with robust security measures.

The prototype emphasizes a user-friendly interface and intuitive navigation, ensuring a seamless experience for users. Explore the Figma prototype to see the detailed design and functionality of the Finsight Financial App.

Food Waste Management App

This prototype showcases a comprehensive solution for managing food waste effectively. The app is designed to help users track, manage, and reduce food waste through various features and functionalities.

Key Features:

  • Food Inventory Management: Users can keep track of their food items, including expiration dates, to minimize waste.
  • Waste Tracking: The app allows users to log and monitor the amount of food waste generated, providing insights into waste patterns.
  • Recipe Suggestions: Based on the available ingredients, the app suggests recipes to help users utilize their food items before they expire.
  • Donation Integration: Users can easily find nearby food donation centers and contribute excess food to those in need.
  • Educational Resources: The app provides tips and articles on reducing food waste and promoting sustainable practices.

The prototype includes intuitive navigation and user-friendly interfaces, ensuring a seamless experience for users. Explore the Figma prototype to see the detailed design and functionality of the Food Waste Management App.

Weather App UI/UX Design

This prototype showcases a user-friendly weather application designed to provide real-time weather updates and forecasts. The app is crafted to deliver a seamless user experience with intuitive navigation and visually appealing interfaces.

Key Features:

  • Current Weather: Displays real-time weather conditions including temperature, humidity, wind speed, and precipitation.
  • Forecast: Provides detailed weather forecasts for the next 7 days, helping users plan their activities accordingly.
  • Location-based Services: Automatically detects the user's location to provide accurate weather updates for their area.
  • Search Functionality: Allows users to search for weather information in different cities and regions.
  • Weather Alerts: Sends notifications for severe weather conditions such as storms, heavy rain, or extreme temperatures.
  • Interactive Maps: Includes interactive weather maps showing radar, satellite, and temperature data.

The prototype emphasizes a clean and modern design, ensuring that users can easily access the information they need. Explore the Figma prototype to see the detailed design and functionality of the Weather App.
